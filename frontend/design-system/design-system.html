Motion Principles

Subtle. Purposeful. Never distracting. Every animation serves clarity.

NameDurationEasingUse
fade-in200msease-outModals, tooltips appearing
fade-up250msease-outContent sections, cards loading
scale-in200msease-outModals, dropdowns, popovers
slide-in-right250msease-outToasts, drawers
slide-in-left250msease-outSidebar drawers
pulse-soft2s loopease-in-outStatus indicators, live data
shimmer1.8s looplinearSkeleton loaders
spinner700ms looplinearLoading spinners

Constraint: Never use bounce, spring, or slide animations for data-dense dashboards. Stick to fade and scale. Respect prefers-reduced-motion by wrapping all animations in a media query.
